Apply for Personal Independence Payment (PIP)

To apply for Personal Independence Payment you need to give details about the effects of your condition or disability. You can ask a friend, family member or support organisation to help you.

Before you start, you'll need:

  • your National Insurance Number
  • your bank or building society details
  • contact details of any doctors, nurses, counsellors or other people who help you
  • details of your medication or treatments
